# Industrial Internship – Neelum Jhelum Hydropower Plant (969 MW)

## 1) Project Overview
This repository presents my industrial internship at the Neelum Jhelum Hydropower Plant (969 MW), where I gained practical experience in the operation, maintenance, and engineering of large-scale hydroelectric power generation systems.
During the internship, I studied the working principles of Francis turbines, auxiliary mechanical systems, compressors, cooling systems, hydraulic equipment, and plant operations while working alongside experienced engineers.

## 2) Objectives
The objectives of this internship were to:
- Understand the operation of a large-scale hydropower plant.
- Study the design and operation of Francis turbines and auxiliary systems.
- Observe maintenance and inspection procedures of mechanical equipment.
- Learn industrial safety practices and engineering documentation.
- Connect theoretical mechanical engineering knowledge with practical industrial applications.
